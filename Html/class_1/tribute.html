Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur known for founding and leading several influential technology companies. Born in South Africa in 1971, he moved to the United States to pursue innovation in software, energy, and space. He co-founded Zip2 and PayPal before creating SpaceX, with the goal of reducing space-travel costs and enabling life on other planets. As Tesla’s CEO, he accelerated the global shift toward electric vehicles and clean energy. Musk has also supported ventures like Neuralink and The Boring Company, focusing on human–computer interaction and transportation tunnels. His bold ideas and ambitious projects have made him one of the most influential figures in modern technology.
